Emergency Medical Response

AfriEmergencies owns its national 24-hour emergency Contact Centre. This facility has the technological capacity to provide a broad range of emergency medical services, including the dispatching of emergency medical resources by air. Our Contact Centre is multifunctional, with tasks ranging from taking calls to evaluating the nature and severity of a situation and dispatching the resources necessary to contain a given situation. Highly trained and dedicated operators, using sophisticated dispatching software, mapping technology and medical databases, manage the centre 24 hours a day. This comprehensive support network enables us to customise solutions to meet our clients’ individual needs.

Concierge Doctor

We recognise that even when you are unwell, it’s important to still feel in control of your life. Going through hospital treatment can naturally be a daunting experience and have an emotional as well as a physical impact. For all less urgent medical complaints and presentations use the AfriEmergencies concierge doctor service which is available via the application or call centre. Our doctors will provide a professional personalised service right at your door- when you call.
